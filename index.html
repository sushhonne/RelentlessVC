When do we invest

WE like to partner with founders early in their journeywhere we can add the most value


How early?

Right at the "I and my co-founder(s) were thinking that market X seems to be broken in a weird way" stage. As an example, one of our first investments was in a startup where the founder still had 8 months of college left!


Cheque Size

The combined cheque size from our network(in 2022) tends to be about $100k - $300k
